PHILOSOPHY

Dr Cousins founded her clinic focused on personalised treatment plans for aesthetic enhancement, meticulously aligned with each individual's unique goals. Her approach involves selecting specific products and techniques that complement the client's skin type, facial structure, teeth and smile.
